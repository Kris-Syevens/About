Welcome to My Portfolio!

My passion for software engineering began when I discovered a need to help people connect with technology. Having worked in the financial services industry and within culturally diverse communities, I realized a great disparity in available resources for Spanish-only speakers compared to English speakers. Taking matters into my own hands, I sought the necessary knowledge and built an online mortgage application website with its content in Spanish to help the needs of my community. During my learning journey, I uncovered countless technology and tools available that would enable me to solve some of the world’s largest problems. I haven’t stopped learning and never looked back.
